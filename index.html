Mesotheraphy Injectables

Anti-aging injectable complex for optimal mesotheraphy. The compound is mainly composed of hyaluronic acid (HA) and polynucleotide (PN), which helps anti-aging process with other key materials including peptides, amino acids, vitamins, among others.

The solution is built upon an advanced concept of cytotrophy, which builds an environment that promotes cell growth. It provides rich nutrients, an appropirate PH level, osmotic pressures, and other beneficial conditions for cell reproduction.

Hyaluronic Acid (HA)

Highly water-resistant and dermotropic, HA provides moisturization necessary for anti-aging. To maximize skin elasticity, our injectable uses supreme quality HA optimal for skin cell reproduction with the optimal molecular weight of 1.4 million Daltons.

Peptides

Our injectable contains intense active peptide complex that utilizes the most effective peptide compound technology. The peptide complex not only provides an optimal environment for HA and PN to function, but also guarantees safety insured by pharmaceutical grade amino acid ingredients.

Polynucleotide (PN)

PN is the DNA component of hyaluronic acid and peptide which has high dermotropic qualities. Extracted from salmon testes, PN provides maximum component efficiency, even surpassing PDRN with supplementation of elastic fibers and restoration of water-oil balance.

Collagen Reproduction

Collagen, the component that makes up to 90\% of skin cells, becomes a fibrous cell when cross-linked typically with hydroxyproline, glysine, and proline. Our injectable supports this process by containing the optimal balance of vitamins, minerals, and the amino components' ratio that corresponds to collagen protein.
